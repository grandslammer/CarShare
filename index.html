About

What is CarShare?

Do you want to save money, and help the environment while skipping the strain of relying on public transport to get to and from where you need to go? Then CarShare could be the service for you. CarShare isn’t just about car sharing - we’re also about completely redefining transportation.

Saves you money

It’s believed that in a first-world nation like the USA, the average annual charge and payouts related to owning a car (petrol/diesel, maintenance, cleaning) can cost upwards of $10,000 alone. There’s one clear alternative to the costly expenses of car ownership, and that’s CarShare!

Peace of mind

As the world’s population continues to grow, and population trends change; more-and-more-people will be moving to urban areas. City planners of the future will have to contend with increased demand for parking spaces, building better road networks to sustain the continued flow of automobiles, and the pollution caused from the aforementioned issues. But there is another way, the CarShare way, which aims to remove cars from our roads, help the environment, be kinder to customer’s pockets and still see that you have full access to a car when needs be.
